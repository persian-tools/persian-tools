acceptableKeywords: RegExp = ...
  • Defined in modules/extractCardNumbers/utils.ts:25

Acceptable keywords between numbers are:

  1. Star -> *
  2. Underscore -> _
  3. Dash -> -
  4. Dot -> .
example:
